Year-End Promotion

This critical operation promotes students to the next class and advances the system to the next academic year. This action cannot be undone.

Instructions

  1. Ensure all marks and attendance for the current year are finalized.
  2. The system will automatically promote students from the current year (shown above) to the next calculated year.
  3. Click the button and follow the confirmation prompt carefully.
  4. After success, the system's active year will be updated, and the page will reload automatically.
  5. All previous year's data (marks, attendance, etc.) will be safely archived and will no longer appear on the main dashboard pages.
