Year-End Promotion

This critical operation promotes students and advances the system to the next academic year. This action cannot be undone.

Instructions & Process

  1. Ensure all marks and attendance for the current year are finalized.
  2. The system will automatically find all active students in the "Promote From" year (including old data without a year tag).
  3. Students in the "Final Graduating Class" will have their status changed to `graduated`.
  4. All other students will have their class incremented by 1 and their academic year updated to the "Promote To" year.
  5. The system's global academic year setting will be updated to the new year.
  6. Click the button and type "PROMOTE" in the confirmation box to proceed.
